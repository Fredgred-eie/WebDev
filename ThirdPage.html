Resident Evil Requiem is a 2026 survival horror game developed and published by Capcom. Newest resident Evil game of the franchise of evil residents.

Terraria

Terraria is a 2011 action-adventure video game developed by Re-Logic and published by 505 Games. A sandbox like minecraft but 2D, a amazing game filled with skill and thought.

Terraria

Payday 2

Payday 2 is a cooperative first-person shooter video game developed by Overkill Software and published by 505 Games. A sequel, this game proves the original concept needed more polish and content.

Palworld

Palworld is an upcoming action-adventure, survival, and monster-taming game created and published by Japanese developer Pocketpair. A sort of Pokemon game that isn't locked to one console. Making it the first ever unoffical pokemon game, that all players could play.

Palworld

Overwatch

Overwatch (Retroactively referred to as Overwatch 1 & Overwatch Classic) was a 2016 live service multiplayer first-person shooter video game by Blizzard Entertainment. A hero shooter that has many great characters and gameplay.
